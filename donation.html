About Us

ST JAMES Foundation is a non-profit organization headquartered in Sub-Saharan Africa; Uganda run by former seminarians who met while pursuing priesthood in 2004.It’s a religious based organization with over 70 members 12 of whom are priests serving in Masaka Diocese. The foundation has been registered since 2019 and has had activities dating back to 2014
