Children's Education: Providing Free Education

Provided : Free Education

We are currently providing free education to 15 children.

Health: Mobilize Medical Bills for a sick child

Raised : $5,000 / $7,500

We raised $5,000 for the operation of Namaganda Faith who had Ventricular Septal Defect. The operation was conducted at Mulago Hospital. The campaign was supported by Busoba members, Pan African Pyramid, Jude Ssempuuma, Fr Kalyowa, Hon. Francis Katabazi, Bwanika Derrick, Denis Kabiito and many more.

Environmental conservation: Agronomy services

Worked with : 10,000 Farmers

We worked with over 10,000 farmers through a farmers call center. Planted fruit trees in kyamaganda. We launched a campaign to plant fruit trees and cash crops to promote environmental conservation, nutrition and household incomes.

Relief Items: Relief Aid for Refugees from South Sudan

Provided : Relief Aid

We have provided relief aid for refugees from South Sudan in BIDI-BIDI Refugee settlement that included providing of food items and clothing.

Free medical services

Medical Services : Nebbi District

We conducted free medical services like consultations, test, diagnosis and management of common illnesses, referral, and linkages for better care.

Nutrition Health and Counselling

outreach Program : Nebbi District

We conducted outreaches in Nebbi, Uganda to fight malnutrition and sensitize mothers on nutritional needs of children. The project involved teaching care givers how to screen and assess malnutrition.

Training Youth in Rabbit Farming

Trained : 1000 Youth

We have over the years trained youth in rabbit farming with an aim of improving nutrition, household incomes, rabbits as a source of fertilizer.

About Us

St JAMES Foundation is a non profit organization headquartered in sub Saharan Africa, Uganda that aims to improve lives of the vulnerable poor through quality health and Education, improve rural incomes through promoting sustainable agricultural practices and micro loans, as we promote good environmental practices to mitigate the impact of climate change. We are passionate about what we do and are here to create a better tomorrow.
