About Our Charity

St JAMES Foundation is a non profit organization headquartered in sub Saharan Africa, Uganda that aims to improve lives of the vulnerable poor through quality health and Education, improve rural incomes through promoting sustainable agricultural practices and micro loans, as we promote good environmental practices to mitigate the impact of climate change. We are passionate about what we do and are here to create a better tomorrow.

Core Areas

  • The Foundation aims to address these core areas:

  • 1. Support vulnerable children, youth and mothers have Quality Health through fighting malnutrition, malaria prevalence, sensitization about HIV/AIDS and Covid-19 awareness.

  • 2. Education of poor children. We aim to equip vulnerable children with an education through skilling and scholarships.

  • 3. Microfinance encouraging savings and extending micro loans to the under privileged especially women and youth.

  • 4. Improving Household incomes through agricultural activities by providing advisory and extension services (information) to the last mile small holder farmers, market, and financial linkages through a farmer call center.

  • 5. To undo the effects of climate change by promoting planting of fruit trees and cash crops.
